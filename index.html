Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island is an amazing place to visit. I loved the long sandy beaches, peaceful nature trails, and the tall lighthouse by the water. The sunsets were beautiful, with the sky turning orange and pink. I also saw lots of birds and other wildlife while walking around. Every spot on the island had a nice view, and it felt calm and relaxing.
